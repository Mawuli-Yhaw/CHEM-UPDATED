Welcome to Chem C=C Lab

This is an application that helps you name organic compounds using their molecular and structural formulae with ease. This application can name up to 120 carbons in a chain. All alkanes, alkenes, alkynes, alkanols, and alkanoic acids in this carbon range.

Further naming of organic compounds with substituents and functional groups are in development and will be featured on a premium level.
